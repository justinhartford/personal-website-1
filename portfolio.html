Complete Philosophy

Core Principles: I put users first. I invest in communities, not funnels. I make things I'd personally consume. I favor compounding systems. I choose long-term impact over short-term optics. I treat brand and trust as real assets. I use paid channels to learn. I value impact over activity. I'm data-informed, not paralyzed. I never forget distribution.

How I Collaborate: I work iteratively, share early and publicly (internally), optimize for clarity over cleverness, avoid silos, bias toward action, and seek direct contact with users and reality.

What You Can Expect: Thoughtful work over performative work. Clear communication and visible progress. Respect for users, teammates, and craft. A willingness to experiment, learn, and adapt. A preference for building things that last.
